# Day 1 - CRM Basics
**Date:** May 8, 2026

## 1. What is CRM
CRM means Customer Relationship Management. It is a system that helps companies store customer data, track interactions, and manage sales processes. CRM improves customer service and increases sales by keeping all customer information in one place.

## 2. Why companies use Salesforce
Salesforce is the number 1 CRM in the world because:
- It is cloud based with no installation needed
- All teams can work together in one platform
- It is easy to customize without coding
- It provides reports and dashboards in real time
- It is used by companies of all sizes globally

## 3. Explain Key Objects
- **Account**: A company or organization. Example: Apollo Hospital, Capgemini
- **Contact**: A person who works at an Account. Example: Doctor from Apollo, Manager from Capgemini  
- **Opportunity**: A running sales deal with amount and stage. Example: ₹5L deal with Apollo Hospital
- **Lead**: A new person who showed interest. Example: Website enquiry, phone call

## 4. Business Flow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

**Explanation**: A Lead is first enquiry. After qualification it becomes Contact. When ready to buy we create Opportunity. After payment they become Customer.

## 5. Real-World Mapping - Hospital System
- **Account**: Apollo Hospital
- **Lead**: Patient who called for enquiry
- **Contact**: Patient who visited and registered
- **Opportunity**: Patient agreed for ₹2L surgery
- **Customer**: Patient after surgery and bill payment
